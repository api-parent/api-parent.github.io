Monthly Consulting

Highly Qualified and Experienced Doctors

V & U Parent Medicare helps the parents and NRIs to choose highly qualified and experienced doctors.

Scheduling & Following-up

Scheduling and Following up with the Doctors and Hospitals for the elderly parents is a mundane task and many a times they fail to meet the Doctor or turn up in the Hospital for appointment. V & U Parent Medicare helps them to overcome this problem by notifying the parents and designated relatives earlier and ensuring that they visit the Doctor / Hospitals on the correct date for their treatment. We also arrange for Second Opinion whenever needed.

Medical Jargons are difficult to comprehend, hence the aging parents and NRIs are put into stress and it creates a panic situation. V & U Parent Medicare’s Panel Doctors eases the pain by giving their opinion and counselling and helps the parents to take the right course of action.

Alerts & Reminders

Timely alerts and reminders will be sent to parents, NRIs and designated close relatives in India through email and SMS.

Standardized Rates

Medical expenses are growing day by day and this is a major cause of worry for the NRIs especially when their Parents have to be treated, when most of the Tier-2 / Tier-3 city Hospitals don’t have proper medical facilities or Doctors. V & U Parent Medicare comes to the rescue of the NRIs by getting their parents to enroll in state of the art Hospitals where highly experienced professionals work. V & U Parent Medicare has signed up Standard & Special Rates with these Hospitals.

Regular Updates

During the treatment process the NRIs do not have a clear visibility about the treatment carried out to their parents. We at V & U Parent Medicare, understand the problem and the anxiety of NRIs. Our experienced Doctors and Consultants work hand-in-hand with the professionals in the Hospitals where the Parents are getting treated and updates the NRIs about the treatment and the condition of their Parents.

Guidance

V & U Parent Medicare guides its clients to get best possible medical insurance and also to buy required medical devices at reasonable cost.

Medical Records

V & U Parent Medicare maintains the medical records in cloud so that the Parents & NRIs can access it from anywhere in a secured way.
