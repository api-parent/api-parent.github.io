Welcome To V & U Parent Medicare

V & U Parent Medicare Pvt. Ltd. is a company founded by a physician with more than 36 years of experience both as a critical care physician and Hospital Administrator. V & U stands for Virtually You and also We and You.

Working Hours :
Mon to Sat – 8 AM to 6 PM (IST)

Mission

To provide sustaining medical guidance and arrange for solution for the medical problems of parents of Non-Resident Indians through the services of Hi-Tech medical centres in India.

To provide online access to the medical history and services through the patient portals

To provide relief to the anxiety of NRIs concerning their parents well being
